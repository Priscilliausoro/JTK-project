Our Services

JTK GLOBAL CONSULT are a trusted leader in integrated travel and real estate solutions in Nigeria. With deep expertise in both industries, we offer excellence, reliability, and customer-centric services.


At JTK GLOBAL CONSULT, we are committed to exceptional service, value creation, and being your trusted partner in Nigeria's travel and real estate sectors.

Travels and Tours

Our travel experts go beyond simply booking flights and accommodations. They curate customized tour packages designed to create unique and unforgettable experiences for our clients. Whether it's exploring cultural landmarks, embarking on adventurous excursions, or indulging in leisurely getaways, we ensure that every aspect of the journey is carefully crafted to exceed expectations.

Visa Application

Navigating the complexities of visa applications can be daunting, but our experienced team is here to provide guidance and support every step of the way. From initial consultations to document preparation and submission, we ensure that the visa application process is smooth, stress-free, and leads to successful outcomes for our clients.

Flight Bookings

Leveraging our extensive network and expertise, we not only secure competitive prices for flights but also provide flexible options that cater to the specific needs and preferences of travelers. This not only saves time and money but also ensures a hassle-free booking experience.

Property Survey

Our thorough property surveys are conducted by experienced professionals to provide valuable insights into property condition, market value, and investment potential. This empowers our clients to make informed decisions and navigate the real estate market with confidence.

Short-let Booking

For travelers seeking short-term accommodations, we offer fully furnished and well-equipped properties. Our short-lets bookings provide convenience and flexibility, allowing travelers to enjoy a comfortable stay without compromising on quality or amenities.

Sales of Property

Our diverse portfolio of properties is carefully curated and vetted to align with the varying needs and investment objectives of our clients. Whether you're looking for residential, commercial, or industrial properties, we have options that are meticulously selected to match your requirements.

Renting and Leasing

Our rental services are designed to facilitate seamless transactions for both landlords and tenants. We focus on maximizing occupancy rates and rental yields, ensuring that properties are efficiently managed to generate optimal returns for our clients.

Change of Title

Handling legal processes such as title transfers and property documentation is crucial in real estate transactions. We ensure compliance with regulations and safeguard our clients' interests throughout the change of title process, providing peace of mind and security.
